About Me

Haonan Huang received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Information at Beijing Institute of Technology, Zhuhai, China (ZHBIT) in 2018, and is currently pursuing the M.S. degree with Prof. Zuyuan Yang in School of Automation at Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China (GDUT) from 2018. Here is Huang's China Software Developer Network (CSDN) blog.

Former leader of the research team (KeYuan Team) in ZHBIT. He is also a Certified LabVIEW Associate Developer(CLAD). In classic music, Huang is good at playing the violin. Now he is a member of Postgraduate Association Orchestra in School of Automation GDUT and as a former member of the ZHBIT Student Orchestra.

E-mail: libertyhhn AT foxmail.com, haonanhuang AT mail2.gdut.edu.cn

Motto: One day, in retrospect, the years of struggle will strike you as the most beautiful.

Research Interests

    His research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition. Now he focuses on the study of Non-negative Matrix Factorization(NMF) and Deep Matrix Factorization (DMF), and their application in data classification/clustering and multi-view learning.
