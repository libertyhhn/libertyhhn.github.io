About Me

Haonan Huang is currently a postdoctoral researcher at RIKEN AIP, advised by Qibin Zhao. From 2021 to 2024, he pursued his Ph.D. under the supervision of Prof. Guoxu Zhou at the School of Automation, Guangdong University of Technology (GDUT), Guangzhou, China. During this time, he was also a jointly-trained Ph.D. student at RIKEN AIP from 2023 to 2024, working with Prof. Qibin Zhao, with support from the China Scholarship Council. Additionally, he served as a visiting scholar at Lehigh University, Bethlehem, U.S., from 2022 to 2023, collaborating with Prof. Lifang He.
