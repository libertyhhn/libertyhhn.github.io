About Me

Haonan Huang received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Information at Beijing Institute of Technology, Zhuhai, China (ZHBIT) in 2018, and received his M.S. degree from the Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China (GDUT) in 2021, under the guidance of Prof. Zuyuan Yang. Now he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with Prof. Guoxu Zhou and Prof. Qibin Zhao in the School of Automation at GDUT from 2021. He also works closely with Prof. Shengli Xie and Prof. Zhenni Li . Here is Huang's China Software Developer Network (CSDN) blog.

He was a Certified LabVIEW Associate Developer (CLAD). In classic music, Huang is good at playing the violin. Now he is a member of Postgraduate Association Orchestra in School of Automation GDUT and as a former member of the ZHBIT Student Orchestra.

E-mail: libertyhhn AT foxmail.com, haonanhuang AT mail2.gdut.edu.cn

Motto: One day, in retrospect, the years of struggle will strike you as the most beautiful.

Research Interests

    His research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition. Now he focuses on the study of Tensor Learning, Low-rank Models, Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) and Deep Matrix Factorization (DMF), and their application in data classification/clustering and multi-view learning.

  • Prof. Kasai has released an NMF codes library, which contains most of the classic NMF models, very helpful for beginners of NMF.

  • Dr. Liang has released a Multi-view open source project, , called "Multi-view learning methods with code", very helpful for beginners of Multi-view Learning.
