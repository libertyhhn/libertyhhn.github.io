About Me

Haonan Huang received his B.Eng. degree from the School of Information at Beijing Institute of Technology, Zhuhai, China (ZHBIT) in 2018, and received his M.S. degree from the Guangdong University of Technology, Guangzhou, China (GDUT) in 2021, under the guidance of Prof. Zuyuan Yang. Now he is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with Prof. Guoxu Zhou and Prof. Qibin Zhao in the School of Automation at GDUT from 2021. Here is Huang's China Software Developer Network (CSDN) blog.

He is also a Certified LabVIEW Associate Developer (CLAD). In classic music, Huang is good at playing the violin. Now he is a member of Postgraduate Association Orchestra in School of Automation GDUT and as a former member of the ZHBIT Student Orchestra.

E-mail: libertyhhn AT foxmail.com, haonanhuang AT mail2.gdut.edu.cn

Motto: One day, in retrospect, the years of struggle will strike you as the most beautiful.

Research Interests

    His research interests include computer vision and pattern recognition. Now he focuses on the study of Tensor Decomposition (TD), Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) and Deep Matrix Factorization (DMF), and their application in data classification/clustering and multi-view learning.
    Prof. Kasai has made a NMF code library, which contains most of the classic NMF code, very helpful for beginners.
