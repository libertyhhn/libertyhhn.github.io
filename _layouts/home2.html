About Me

I am currently a Professor at School of Automation, Guangdong University of Technology.

I obtained my Ph.D degree from the South China University of Technology in 2009, working closely with Profs. Shengli Xie, Shuxue Ding and Yong Xiang.

zuyuanyang AT gmail.com, yangzuyuan AT gdut.edu.cn

Research Interests

  • Sparse Representation(SR), Blind Source Separation(BSS), Nonnegative Matrix Factorization(NMF)
  • Parallel Intelligence (PI)/Artifitial Intelligence (AI)
  • Applications of signal/image processing methods in PI/AI

    • Every year we have a number of places to recruit post graduates and Ph.D students. If you think you are highly motivated and have strong interest in image/signal processing and machine learning algorithm, please feel free to send me an email to discuss the opportunities. It does not matter whether you are a graduate student or just an undergraduate; as long as you are willing to work hard, you are welcomed to join my research team!
